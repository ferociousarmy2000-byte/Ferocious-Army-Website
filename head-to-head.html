Head to Head (H2H) Mastery

Win duels with tempo control, smart pressing, and match-up awareness

Start Learning

Overview

Head to Head (H2H) is a competitive mode where real-time decisions matter. Your success depends on managing momentum, timing tackles/pressing, and choosing the right build-up lanes.

This guide focuses on repeatable patterns: spacing discipline, quick counter triggers, and defensive stability so you can convert chances under pressure.

Key Gameplay Principles

1) Control the Midfield

Cut passing lanes, keep your midfield compact, and force the opponent into wide, low-danger attacks.

2) Trigger Counters at the Right Time

After regaining possession, move forward quickly—don’t wait for perfect chances if the lanes open early.

3) Defend with Patience

Avoid panic tackles. Press smartly and maintain shape to reduce through-ball opportunities.

Attacking & Defending Rules

Attacking Rules
  • Don't sprint all the time.
  • Sprint only in open space.
  • Use short passes and triangles.
  • Build through midfield.
  • Use one-twos near the box.
  • Don't force through balls.
  • Let the ball move faster than players.
Defending Rules
  • Defend with CDM first.
  • Don't drag CBs out.
  • Force the opponent wide.
  • Use your second defender carefully.
  • Protect the centre and edge of the box.
  • Stay patient, don't spam tackles.
